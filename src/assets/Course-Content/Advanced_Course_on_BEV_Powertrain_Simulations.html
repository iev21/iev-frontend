Advanced Battery Electric Vehicle Powertrain Simulations

Course Description:

This advanced-level course provides a comprehensive and in-depth study of powertrain simulations for battery electric vehicles (BEVs). Students will explore advanced modeling techniques, detailed component analysis, and optimization strategies. The course will involve extensive use of simulation tools and software to develop, analyze, and optimize BEV powertrains.

Course Objectives:

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

  • Master advanced simulation techniques for BEV powertrains.
  • Develop high-fidelity models of BEV components.
  • Perform comprehensive performance analysis and optimization of BEV powertrains.
  • Utilize cutting-edge simulation software for BEV powertrain development.
  • Integrate multi-domain simulations for holistic BEV analysis.

Course Outline:

Module 1: Overview of Advanced BEV Powertrain Simulation

  • Introduction to advanced simulation methodologies
  • Review of basic powertrain concepts
  • Importance of detailed simulations in BEV design

Module 2: High-Fidelity Modeling of Electric Motors

  • Advanced motor modeling techniques
  • Dynamic and transient behavior modeling
  • Simulation of motor types and configurations

Module 3: Advanced Battery Modeling

  • Electrochemical and thermal modeling of batteries
  • Simulation of battery dynamics and performance

Module 4: Power Electronics and Control Systems

  • Detailed modeling of inverters, converters, and control systems
  • Simulation of power electronic interactions
  • Advanced control strategies for powertrain optimization

Module 5: Transmission and Drivetrain Dynamics

  • Modeling and simulation of transmission systems
  • Impact of drivetrain dynamics on overall performance
  • Simulation of mechanical losses and efficiency

Module 6: Multi-Physics Simulation Approaches

  • Integration of electrical, thermal, and mechanical domains
  • Coupled simulations for comprehensive analysis
  • Software tools for multi-physics simulations

Module 7: Energy Management and Optimization

  • Advanced energy management strategies
  • Optimization algorithms for powertrain efficiency
  • Simulation of real-time energy management systems

Module 8: Thermal Management and Simulation

  • Advanced thermal modeling techniques
  • Simulation of cooling and heating systems
  • Impact of thermal management on performance and safety

Module 9: Real-World Driving Cycles and Scenarios

  • Simulation of standardized driving cycles
  • Analysis of real-world driving data
  • Impact of driving behavior on powertrain performance

Module 10: Simulation Tools and Software

  • Overview of advanced simulation softwares
  • Setting up complex simulations
  • Advanced features and capabilities of simulation tools

Module 11: Case Studies and Advanced Applications

  • Case studies of advanced BEV powertrain simulations
  • Analysis of simulation results from real-world applications
  • Lessons learned and best practices

Module 12: Future Trends and Innovations

  • Emerging technologies in BEV powertrain simulation
  • Role of AI and machine learning in simulation and optimization
  • Future directions and industry trends

Assessment:

  • Quizzes and assignments to reinforce advanced concepts
  • Midterm report covering detailed modeling and simulation techniques
  • Assignments: Periodic assignments including readings, discussions, and practical activities.
  • Participation: Active participation in discussions and activities.
  • Final exam to assess comprehensive understanding of course material
